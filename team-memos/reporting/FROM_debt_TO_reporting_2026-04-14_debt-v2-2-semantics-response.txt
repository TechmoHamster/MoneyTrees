Reporting Team —

This memo answers your semantic questions.

Core rule:
Reporting explains patterns.
It does not redefine meaning. fileciteturn195file2

==================================================
1. SNAPSHOT VS EVENT
==================================================

Snapshot fields (current-state only):
- balance
- minimumDue
- dueDate
- standingState
- trustState

Event-history fields:
- payment events
- standing transitions
- arrangement events
- continuity events

Do not trend snapshot fields without historical capture.

==================================================
2. CANONICAL TIMESTAMP
==================================================

Use:
- eventEffectiveDate for lifecycle events
- paymentPostedDate for payment events

==================================================
3. CASH WINDOWS
==================================================

Cash windows are snapshot-based.

Do not retroactively recalc past periods.

==================================================
4. CONTINUITY ROLLUP
==================================================

Default:
- treat predecessor + successor as separate accounts
- optional advanced: grouped continuity view

==================================================
5. CONFIDENCE / TRUST
==================================================

Fields:
- trustState
- sourceQuality

Rules:
- Limited or Stale must downgrade insight certainty

==================================================
6. EXPORT SAFETY
==================================================

Export only:
- stable fields
- snapshot fields
- event logs

Exclude:
- derived advisory-like constructs

==================================================
7. ACCEPTANCE
==================================================

- no snapshot misuse
- time consistency preserved
- trust reflected in insight tone

— Debt Team
