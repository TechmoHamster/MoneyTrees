Subject: Debt Section Introduction and Overview Next Steps

Overview Team —

Debt is now an approved product section within MoneyTrees.

Debt exists as a new upstream truth source for debt-related data and will begin feeding structured debt signals into the system once built.

==================================================
1. OVERVIEW ROLE REMINDER
==================================================

Overview is the visibility and prioritization layer.

Overview exists to:
- surface current financial state
- show what needs attention first through presentation
- highlight what changed
- route the user to the correct section

Overview does not:
- define financial truth
- perform analysis
- provide recommendations

Debt will now provide additional truth for Overview to surface.

==================================================
2. WHAT OVERVIEW SHOULD EXPECT FROM DEBT
==================================================

Debt will provide structured signals such as:
- total active debt
- next upcoming debt payments
- high utilization indicators
- debt share of obligations
- presence of debt-related states (delinquent, deferred, etc.)

These should be treated as factual signals, not interpreted insights.

==================================================
3. OVERVIEW NEXT STEPS AFTER CODEX OUTPUT
==================================================

Once Codex delivers the Debt section:

1. Review Debt outputs
- confirm what signals are available
- identify which signals are appropriate for Overview

2. Integrate Debt signals into Overview surfaces
- surface key debt facts clearly
- ensure signals are compact and scannable
- avoid duplication across panels

3. Maintain prioritization through presentation only
- use layout and ordering to highlight importance
- do not introduce new logic or scoring

4. Route users correctly
- provide clear entry points into Debt
- avoid trapping users inside Overview

==================================================
4. OVERVIEW GUARDRAILS
==================================================

When integrating Debt:
- do not define new urgency logic
- do not define new thresholds
- do not reinterpret debt meaning
- do not add advisory language
- do not duplicate Debt detail views

Overview should surface the signal.
Atlas interprets it.

==================================================
5. ESCALATION CONDITIONS
==================================================

Flag immediately if:
- debt signals are unclear or ambiguous
- Overview is forced to interpret meaning instead of display it
- duplication appears between Overview and Debt
- local urgency logic is required to make Debt signals usable

==================================================
6. WORKING EXPECTATION
==================================================

Overview should treat Debt like any other upstream truth source:
- surface it clearly
- prioritize visually
- route appropriately
- do not reinterpret it

— Debt Team
