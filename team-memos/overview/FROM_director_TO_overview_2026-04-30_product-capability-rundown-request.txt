Subject: Product Capability Rundown Request

Overview Team —

I am requesting a focused product rundown of Overview in its current form.

Purpose:
We are moving toward treating MoneyTrees as a fuller financial product, not just a dashboard shell or summary page.

That means I need a clean, honest read on what Overview currently is, what it can already do well, what it cannot do yet, and what product reality another team or future engineering pass needs to understand immediately.

What I need back from Overview:

1. Current Overview capabilities
Provide a grounded rundown of what Overview can currently do across:
- top-level financial state visibility
- KPI / summary visibility
- attention signals
- routing into deeper sections
- compact insight surfaces
- relationship to Atlas on the page
- low-data and sparse-data behavior

2. Current Overview limits
List what Overview does NOT do yet, should not be assumed to do, or still handles only partially.

3. Current product dependencies / integrations
Explain Overview’s current relationship with:
- Bills
- Reporting
- Atlas
- Debt

4. User-facing reality
Explain what a real user can currently rely on Overview for today versus what still feels incomplete, unstable, beta-like, or in need of refinement.

5. Full-product future relevance
Given the goal of a fuller financial product, identify what Overview would need in order to better support:
- full financial-state visibility
- subscriptions context if applicable
- debt visibility
- budgeting visibility if budgeting becomes part of the product
- cleaner command-center behavior for daily use

6. Risks / warnings
Flag any areas where people might overestimate Overview, misunderstand its role, or expect it to perform advisory/analysis work that should remain elsewhere.

Response standard:
- be honest
- be concrete
- do not oversell
- do not speak aspirationally when the feature is not truly present
- distinguish clearly between current reality and future opportunity

team-memos/director/
