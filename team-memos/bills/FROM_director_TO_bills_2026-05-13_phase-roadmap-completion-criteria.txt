DIRECTOR MEMO

TOPIC: Phase Roadmap Completion Criteria — Phases 1-5

==================================================
1. BILLS ROLE ACROSS ALL PHASES
==================================================

Bills remains the operational truth layer for obligations and payments.

Bills owns:
- obligation records
- payment records
- recurring structures
- fee tracking
- partial payment truth
- variable bill handling
- obligation/payment relationships

Bills does not become Atlas, Overview, Reporting, or Debt.

==================================================
2. PHASE RESPONSIBILITIES
==================================================

PHASE 1 — Financial Survival + Core Usability

Tasks:
- Build reliable obligation tracking.
- Support recurring and one-time bills.
- Support partial payments, late fees, remaining balances, and past-due states.
- Support subscriptions, debt payments, loans, BNPL installments, and variable bills.
- Support unemployed/no-income use cases.
- Ensure Bills can power Overview, Debt, Reporting, and Atlas with clean truth.

Complete when:
- Users can track obligations and payments reliably.
- Past-due and unpaid obligations are clear.
- Debt-linked obligations preserve traceability.
- Variable bills and partial payments work cleanly.
- Bills can support debt-pressure workflows.

PHASE 2 — Full Budgeting Platform

Tasks:
- Support all budgeting methods cleanly.
- Improve recurring template logic.
- Improve grouping/filtering/search.
- Support envelope/category relationships where needed.
- Support budgeting-cycle flexibility.
- Improve bulk management workflows.

Complete when:
- Bills can operate cleanly under multiple budgeting methods.
- Users can manage obligations at scale.
- Recurring and variable workflows feel operationally stable.

PHASE 3 — Financial Intelligence Layer

Tasks:
- Expose cleaner analytical metadata to Atlas and Reporting.
- Preserve obligation/payment history for scenario analysis.
- Support clearer state transitions.
- Improve field completeness visibility.

Complete when:
- Bills truth supports trustworthy Atlas analysis.
- Historical payment/obligation state changes are inspectable.
- Bills remains authoritative and auditable.

PHASE 4 — Full Financial Ecosystem

Tasks:
- Support linked financial accounts.
- Improve recurring detection.
- Support imported obligation/payment matching.
- Support subscription/service/provider relationships.
- Improve cross-product consistency.

Complete when:
- Bills can integrate with broader account ecosystems.
- Imported data preserves traceability.
- Manual and imported records coexist cleanly.

PHASE 5 — Platform + Automation

Tasks:
- Support automation hooks safely.
- Preserve auditability of automated changes.
- Support automation review and rollback.
- Preserve source-of-truth integrity during automation.

Complete when:
- Automated bill/payment updates remain inspectable.
- Users can verify what changed and why.
- Bills truth remains trustworthy under automation.

==================================================
3. GLOBAL COMPLETION RULE
==================================================

No phase is complete for Bills unless:

- obligation truth is reliable
- payment truth is traceable
- recurring logic is stable
- fee/partial-payment handling is inspectable
- debt-linked records remain consistent
- Bills preserves clean operational ownership
