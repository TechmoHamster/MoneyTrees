3. BILLS INTEGRATION CONTRACT — IMPLEMENTED
==================================================

The Bills integration follows the approved contract.

Implemented behavior:
- users create and manage debt accounts in Debt
- Bills receives only debt-linked operational obligation rows derived from Debt
- only near-term debt obligations inside a 60-day forward window are materialized operationally
- debt-linked bill rows remain tied to their source debt account
- Bills rows linked to Debt are treated as derived operational rows, not independent debt accounts

Guardrails implemented:
- Debt-linked rows are visibly labeled as linked to Debt
- editing a debt-linked row in Bills redirects the user to the source debt account in Debt instead of allowing structural bill editing
- deleting a debt-linked row from Bills is blocked
- payment capture and late-fee operational handling still remain possible in Bills

This preserves the ownership split:
- Debt owns account structure and debt math
- Bills owns operational payment capture
