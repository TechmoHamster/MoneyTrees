Debt V2.2 scenario coverage added

Scenarios covered in lib/debt.test.ts:
1. Partial payment remains short of cure and standing stays unsatisfied.
2. Payment initiated then failed; standing progression continues and conflict stays visible.
3. Hardship active with interest accrual and standing freeze.
4. Promo APR expiry applies from its effective date forward only.
5. Student-loan deferment ending with capitalization recalculates future interest from the larger principal.
6. Balance-transfer continuity suppresses predecessor projection and avoids double-counting.
7. Delinquent standing and dispute overlay coexist without flattening either state.
8. Payment completed in Bills then reversed removes satisfaction effect and recalculates standing.
9. Manual collections confirmation produces severe-state lifecycle facts with source-quality visibility.
